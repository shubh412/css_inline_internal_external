Ingredients

  • Slices of bread

  • Butter, softened

  • Cheese slices

  • Tomato, patted dry

  • Slices crisply cooked bacon

  • Spinach leaves

Instructions

  1. Spread 1 side of each bread slice with 1/2 tablespoon butter.

  2. Layer 1 slice cheese, 2 slices tomato, 2 slices bacon, 3 to 4 spinach leaves and 1 slice cheese onto each of 4 slices bread, buttered-side down.

  3. Heat 10-inch skillet over medium heat.

  4. Place 1 sandwich into skillet; cook, turning once, 8-10 minutes or until golden brown and cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining sandwiches.
